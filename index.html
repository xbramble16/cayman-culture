Food is an important part of Caymanian culture. Popular foods in the Cayman Islands are Cayman Style Beef which is very favorful and well-seasoned. Another one is Cayman Style Fish which is most commonly made with a fish called snapper. Another favorite is rice and beans which a popular side with most dishes. Additionally, another very popular side with dishes is breadkind which tends to be very heavy. Breadkind consists of dumplins, potatoes, and yams. Conch Fritters are also a popular seafood dish. The conch is marinated in a type of seasoned oil and fried. The most favorited side all over the Caribbean is fried plantain. People will fight for this side because of its sweet taste.

Music is universal. In the Caribbean, everyone listens to very similar music. There's different genres that are popular and originated in different islands. Soca originated in Trinidad & Tobago. Soca is a music genre that stems from Calypso. Dancehall originated in Jamaica. Dancehall is a music genre that stems from Reggae. Soca and Dancehall are the two modern ones that are very popular among teenagers.

Batabano is a huge annual carnival that's held in the first week in May in George Town, Grand Cayman. CayMAS is another annual carnival that takes place in May. The goal of this festival is become a tourism product. Both Batabano and CayMAS are carnivals where the women are dressed feathers. Pirates' Week is a very popular annual festival that lasts for 5 days starting in the 2nd week of November. At this festival, a lot of people dress up as pirates. This festival celebrates when pirates arrived on the islands. Braccanal is an annual festival that takes place on the sister island of Cayman Brac. This festival lasts for 5 days in the 3rd week of May. In the Cayman Islands, May is the month of celebrations, carnivals and parades.

Crafts is an important part of the culture. Baskets and rope are traditionally woven from dried silver thatch palm leaves. Only a select few of people know how to weave. A lot of teenagers and children don't know how to do these crafts. Diving is a commercial activity. Diving is most popular in Little Cayman because it's so isolated and not as busy as Grand Cayman. Diving is done by residents and tourists alike.
