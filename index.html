Food is an important part of Caymanian culture. Popular foods in the Cayman Islands are Cayman Style Beef which is very favorful and well-seasoned. Another one is Cayman Style Fish which is most commonly made with a fish called snapper. Another favorite is rice and beans which a popular side with most dishes. Additionally, another very popular side with dishes is breadkind which tends to be very heavy. Breadkind consists of dumplins, potatoes, and yams. Conch Fritters are also a popular seafood dish. The conch is marinated in a type of seasoned oil and fried. The most favorited side all over the Caribbean is fried plantain. People will fight for this side because of its sweet taste.
